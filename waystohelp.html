Home Delivery Driver

During Thanksgiving, many people are unable to go out and get the food and ingredients that they need in order to have a proper meal. You can help end this problem by signing up to be a home delivery driver! By driving around and delivering food to those in need, you not only help your community, but also yourself!

Cooking and Meal Prep

You can help out those less fortunate than yourself by cooking and/or planning out step by step meal plans for them. You could also go and volunteer at a soup kitchen or a community meal center if you are inexperienced but still want to help.

Feed a Family

You can contribute to the joy of others this Thanksgiving by donating money. Your funds can go towards volunteer efforts like all the ones mentioned before this.

Donate Food

If you can't volunteer this year but still want to help, consider donating some Thanksgiving foods to your local food bank. This would not only provide immense help and happiness to families, but also give you a sense of responsibility and importance this holiday.
