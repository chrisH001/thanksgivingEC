Thanksgiving

How you can give back this holiday

Find a Food Bank In Your Community

You can use the Feeding America website to find volunteering opportunities in your area!

Sign Up For a Volunteer Shift

Most food banks list their available volunteer shifts on their website. By taking advantage of these resources you can help volunteer this Thanksgiving!

Show Up And Have Fun

Volunteering can be more exciting than you think! Help out and enjoy yourself this Thanksgiving!

Food Sorting And Packaging

During the holidays, many people aren't able to afford the luxuries most other people can. For example, many people may not be able to purchase all if any food items on Thanksgiving. As a result, many volunteer programs like the one shown above allow those who are less fortunate to have the opportunity to experience a high quality Thanksgiving meal.

Shopping Assistance

Even if families are able to afford groceries, many of them contain disabled, sickly, or old relatives, meaning they are unable to go out a shop for themselves. As a result, you can help out by volunteering to go out and shop for them! Grab a cart, head on down to your local grocery store, and help out a family this Thanksgiving!
